Accessibility VR Experience

An immersive road crossing simulation designed for people with physical disabilities to practice real-world scenarios safely

About This Simulation

This VR experience is designed to help people with physical disabilities practice crossing roads in a safe, controlled environment. The simulation includes realistic elements like:

  • Accessibility ramps on sidewalks
  • Wheelchair-friendly pathways
  • Controlled traffic patterns
  • Accessible public spaces with tables and chairs
  • Realistic street lighting for visibility

Users can practice navigating this environment to build confidence and skills for real-world situations without physical risk.

How It Helps

For people with mobility challenges, everyday tasks like crossing a road can present significant obstacles. This simulation allows users to:

  • Practice timing when to cross with moving traffic
  • Learn to navigate accessibility features like ramps
  • Gain confidence in a risk-free environment
  • Develop spatial awareness in crowded areas
